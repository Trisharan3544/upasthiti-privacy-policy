Features

Everything a teacher needs

Built for independent teachers, coaching institutes, and schools — every major capability shipping in the current app.

👆
Swipe attendance
Swipe right for Present, left for Absent. Faster than paper registers for daily roll call.
✅
Bulk mark & holiday
One-tap All Present, All Absent, or mark the whole class as Holiday when needed.
🏫
Classes & departments
Create and organize classes, departments, and student roll lists in one clean workflow.
📷
Student photos
Optional student photos via the Android system picker — stored securely in Firebase Storage.
📥
Excel & list import
Bulk-import students from Excel files or text lists. Undo a mistaken import when available.
📅
History & past dates
Revisit attendance history. Editing a past date on free tier can be unlocked with an optional rewarded ad.
✔️
Completion indicators
See when attendance for a class/date is complete so you never leave a roll half-done.
📊
PDF & CSV reports
Monthly and custom date-range reports with attendance percentages — export PDF or CSV.
☁️
Offline + cloud sync
Mark attendance without internet. Data syncs securely to Google Firebase when you reconnect.
🔔
Reminders & push
Optional local attendance reminders plus Firebase Cloud Messaging push alerts for updates.
🏛️
Institute workspace
Personalize with your class, coaching institute, school, or academy name (shown on profile and reports).
🔐
Secure email sign-in
Email/password accounts with email verification before full access. Optional phone on your profile.
↩️
Undo & restore
Undo accidental deletes or imports where supported, and restore Pro purchases from Google Play.
💬
Feedback & share
Send in-app feedback, rate on Google Play, and refer a friend with a one-tap share link.
🛡️
Privacy controls
In-app Privacy Policy & Terms, ad consent (UMP where required), and Delete Account & Data.

How it works

Up and running in minutes

No training required. If you can use WhatsApp, you can use Upasthiti.

01
Download & verify email
Install from Google Play, register with email/password, and verify your email for full access.
02
Create classes & import
Add classes and students manually, or import from Excel / lists to save time.
03
Mark attendance

04
Export reports
Track summaries and export attendance reports in PDF and CSV when you need them.
Privacy

Your data, your control

We take student data seriously. Here is what matters most — full details are in our Privacy Policy (updated July 18, 2026).

📧
Account & email
Email and password for sign-in, with email verification before full app access. Optional phone on your profile. Not sold to advertisers.
🗃️
Attendance & students
Stored securely in Firebase under your account. Only you can access your classes, students, and attendance.
🔔
Notifications
Optional local reminders and FCM push alerts. You control reminder settings; notification permission is requested where required.
💳
Upasthiti Pro billing
Subscriptions are billed by Google Play. We do not receive your full payment card details — only entitlement status needed to unlock Pro.
🗑️
Data deletion
Delete your account and synced data from Profile > Privacy Settings > Delete Account & Data, or contact us.
📢
Ads (free tier)
Banner, interstitial, app-open, and optional rewarded ads via Google AdMob. Student records are never used for ad targeting. Pro removes ads.
