Privacy

Your data, your control

We take student data seriously. Here’s what we collect and why.

📧
Email address
Email and password (and optional phone verification via Firebase) for sign-in. Not sold to third parties.
🗃️
Attendance data
Stored securely in Firebase Firestore under your account. Only you can access it.
📱
App usage data
Crash reports via Firebase Crashlytics to improve stability — see our full policy for details.
🔒
Encryption
Data is encrypted in transit (HTTPS/TLS) and securely stored by Firebase.
🗑️
Data deletion
You can request full deletion of your account and associated data by contacting us.
🍪
Ads
The free version may show ads via Google AdMob. Manage personalization in device settings.

For the full details, read the Privacy Policy.
